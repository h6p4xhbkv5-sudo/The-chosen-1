Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Synaptiq

What does Synaptiq cover?
Synaptiq covers the complete A-Level Mathematics specification — Pure Maths (Year 1 & Year 2), Statistics, and Mechanics. Every topic from Algebra and Surds through to Integration, Vectors, Hypothesis Testing, and Projectiles. All content is aligned to AQA, Edexcel, OCR, and WJEC exam boards.
Is the free trial really free?
Yes! The 7-day free trial gives you full access to all features — no credit card required. You can cancel at any time before the trial ends and you won't be charged a penny.
How does the AI Maths tutor work?
Synaptiq uses advanced AI trained specifically on the A-Level Maths curriculum. You can ask any maths question and get a detailed, step-by-step explanation with full working — just like a private tutor. The AI knows your exam board, shows proper mathematical notation, and adapts to your level.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. Synaptiq is fully GDPR-compliant and all data is processed and stored securely within the UK/EU. We never sell your data. You can request deletion of all your data at any time from your settings.
Can I use Synaptiq on my phone?
Yes! Synaptiq is fully responsive and works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can study anywhere — on the bus, in the library, or at home.
What accessibility features are available?
Synaptiq includes dedicated modes for ADHD (focus mode, shorter sessions), Dyslexia (special fonts, spacing, audio read-aloud), Dyscalculia (visual maths, number lines), and Autism Spectrum (predictable structure, literal explanations). These can be enabled during signup or at any time from your settings.
Do you offer school licences?
Yes! We offer bulk school licences with teacher dashboards, assignment tracking, and class analytics. Contact us at the pricing section or via our contact page for a custom quote.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, email, learning profile (including any learning difficulties you disclose), and learning activity data including questions answered, tutor conversations, and progress metrics.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to personalise your learning experience. Learning difficulty information is used to adapt the AI tutor, font settings, question formatting, and accessibility features.

3. AI Conversations

Your conversations with the AI tutor are processed via the Anthropic API. These conversations are used to provide responses and are not stored permanently beyond your active session.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, delete your data, export your data, and withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at privacy@synaptiq.co.uk.

5. Data for Minors

For users under 13, we require parental consent. We collect minimal data and do not use it for any purpose beyond providing the educational service.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and optional analytics cookies (which you can decline). We do not use advertising cookies.
