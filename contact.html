Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your Dashboard → Settings → Subscription → Manage Plan. You can cancel at any time. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period.

Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 14 days of your first payment if you're not satisfied. For subsequent months, refunds are not available except where required by UK consumer law. Email billing@luminaai.co.uk to request a refund.

My child is under 13 — is that okay?

Users under 13 require verifiable parental consent. Please email privacy@luminaai.co.uk with your consent and we'll set up the account appropriately with extra privacy protections.

Is the AI always accurate?

Lumina AI uses state-of-the-art AI tuition, but AI can occasionally make mistakes. Always cross-reference with your textbook or teacher for exam-critical information. We're continuously improving accuracy.

Can I use Lumina AI for my whole school?

Yes! We offer school and college licensing with discounted bulk rates, teacher dashboards, and class management tools. Email schools@luminaai.co.uk for a quote.

What subjects does Lumina AI cover?

We cover all major GCSE and A-Level subjects including Mathematics, English Literature, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, Computer Science, and Economics. More subjects are added regularly.

Does the AI do my homework for me?

Lumina AI is designed to help you learn, not to do your work for you. It explains concepts, gives worked examples, and tests your understanding. Using AI to submit work as your own may violate your school's academic integrity policy.
